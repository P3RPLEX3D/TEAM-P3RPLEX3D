Plot

Abby, Hope, Lexi, and Trick Dangers arrive in Mirodahl on Hopes pirate ship. Trick quickly eludes the party informing the guards posted outside the house of the rising sun that "there may just be 3 dangerous fugitives in the harbor" seeming to betray the party. However with the guards now absent trick will be able to gain entry to the mysterious house and steal the Golden Sun that he has been trying to get his hands on.
