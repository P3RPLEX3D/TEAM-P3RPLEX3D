Torkah

Torkah by Takari definition, means literally, "Home". It is a land with the most fertile soil, the tallest mountians and by far the most dangerous natives... by far.

Ragna Wildhammer

Ragna Wildhammer was a man of few words, but what he lacked in words he made up for in rage and brute strength. He was the largest member of his Takarian clan known as "The Iron-Bear", and One of the toughest Takarian to ever set foot on to what was known as a Dragonship.
